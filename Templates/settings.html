Compliance Keywords

These words and phrases are scanned for in every transcript. The tool flags any line that contains them, with surrounding context, in the PDF report. Edit freely — your changes save instantly.

    Built-in Red-Flag Phrases

    These phrases are always scanned and cannot be removed — they indicate potential procedural violations.

    Examples:
    "we can't do that" · "we don't have to" · "that's not our responsibility" · "no funding" · "without consent" · "didn't notify" · "waiting list" · "can't afford"

    Red-flag matches appear in a separate section of the PDF report with full context.

    About the Scanner

    The compliance scanner runs on every transcript after it's generated. It checks each line for keyword matches, records the surrounding context, and includes a flag table in the PDF report.

    Flag categories:
    Regulatory — IDEA, IEP, Section 504, FERPA, etc.
    Red flag — phrases suggesting procedural issues
